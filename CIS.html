KEYWORD_01:ASSET

(ASSET):CIS1.1:Utilize an active discovery tool:Utilize an active discovery tool to identify devices connected to the organization's network and update the hardware asset inventory. Corresponds to:27001A.8.1.1:Inventory of assets:Assets associated with information and information processing facilities shall be identified and an inventory of these assets shall be drawn up and maintained.

(ASSET):CIS1.2:Use a Passive Asset Discovery Tool:Utilize a passive discovery tool to identify devices connected to the organization's network and automatically update the organization's hardware asset inventory.Corresponds to:27001A.8.1.1:Inventory of assets:
Assets associated with information and information processing facilities shall be identified and an inventory of these assets shall be drawn up and maintained.

KEYWORD_02:VULNR

(VULNR):CIS3.1:Run Automated Vulnerability Scanning Tools:Utilize an up-to-date SCAP-compliant vulnerability scanning tool to automatically scan all systems on the network on a weekly or more frequent basis to identify all potential vulnerabilities on the organization's systems. Corresponds to:27001A.12.6.1:*Control
Information about technical vulnerabilities of information systems being used shall be obtained in a timely fashion, the organization’s exposure to such vulnerabilities evaluated and appropriate measures
taken to address the associated risk.

KEYWORD_03:ACCOUNT

(ACCOUNT):CIS4.1Maintain Inventory of Administrative Accounts:Use automated tools to inventory all administrative accounts, including domain and local accounts, to ensure that only authorized individuals have elevated privileges. Corresponds to:27001A.9.2.2:*Control A formal user access provisioning process shall be implemented to assign or revoke access rights for all user types to all systems and services.
